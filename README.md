## 🎯 The Operations Model

We eliminate agency bureaucracy. At Zevron Studio, you bypass account managers, outsourced teams, and fragmented communication channels. 

* **🤝 Direct Founder Engagement:** Our studio is led by two founders. One directs technical engineering and execution; the other manages client strategy and alignment. You interface with the same principal leader from initial scoping to final deployment.
